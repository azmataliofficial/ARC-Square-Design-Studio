Frequently Asked Questions

What services does ARC Square Design Studio offer?

We offer Architecture, Interior Design, Exterior Design, Space Planning, 2D Drafting, and 3D Modeling & Rendering — all tailored to your vision, budget, and requirements.

Do you handle both architecture and interior design?

Yes! ARC Square specializes in both architecture and interior design. We provide end-to-end solutions from structural planning to final interior styling and 3D visualization.

How involved will I be during the design process?

Very involved! We believe in a fully collaborative approach. You will be consulted at every key milestone — from initial concept to final delivery — so the design truly reflects your vision.

Can you work with a specific budget or timeline?

Absolutely. We always begin with understanding your budget and deadline. Our team creates customized design plans that maximize value within your constraints without compromising on quality.

Do you provide 3D visualization before construction?

Yes, we provide detailed 3D renderings and walkthroughs so you can see exactly how your space will look before a single brick is laid. This helps avoid surprises and saves time and money.

When was ARC Square Design Studio founded?

ARC Square Design Studio was founded in 2009 by Md. Arif Saifi with a vision to create spaces that are both functionally excellent and visually inspiring.

Where is your studio located?

Our studio is located at Shop No. 2/24, Ground Floor, B.D.A Complex, M.G Road, Bulandshahr, Uttar Pradesh 203001. We also serve clients across Delhi NCR and pan India remotely.

How many projects have you completed?

Over the past 15+ years, we have successfully completed 380+ projects ranging from residential homes and commercial spaces to large-scale architectural planning projects.

What makes ARC Square different from other studios?

We combine 15+ years of experience with a truly client-first philosophy. Every project gets dedicated attention, personalized design, and a commitment to timely delivery with uncompromised quality.

How is the pricing structured for design projects?

Pricing is based on project scope, area size, and type of service required. We offer transparent, milestone-based payment plans so you always know what you're paying for and when.

Do you offer a free initial consultation?

Yes! We offer a completely free initial consultation. We understand your requirements, share our approach, and provide a project estimate — all before any commitment from your side.

Are revisions included in the project cost?

Minor revisions and adjustments are included in all our packages. For major scope changes, we discuss transparently and provide a revised estimate before proceeding.

Can I get a custom quote for my project?

Absolutely. Every project is unique. Contact us at +91 96509 27987 or arcsquaredesignstudio@gmail.com and we will provide a detailed custom quote tailored to your specific needs.

About Us

Founded in 2009, ARC Square Design Studio has grown from a small boutique firm into one of the region's most respected architectural and design practices. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create spaces that not only look beautiful but also enhance the way people live, work, and interact.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of working on diverse projects ranging from intimate residential renovations to large-scale commercial developments. Each project is an opportunity for us to push creative boundaries while staying true to our core principles of functionality, sustainability, and timeless design.

Today, our team of talented architects, designers, and project managers continues to deliver exceptional results, combining technical expertise with artistic vision to transform dreams into reality
