Our Story

Founded in 2009, ARC Square Design Studio has grown from a small boutique firm into one of the region's most respected architectural and design practices. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create spaces that not only look beautiful but also enhance the way people live, work, and interact.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of working on diverse projects ranging from intimate residential renovations to large-scale commercial developments. Each project is an opportunity for us to push creative boundaries while staying true to our core principles of functionality, sustainability, and timeless design.

Today, our team of talented architects, designers, and project managers continues to deliver exceptional results, combining technical expertise with artistic vision to transform dreams into reality
